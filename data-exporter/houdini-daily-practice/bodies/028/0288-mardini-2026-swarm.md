### Mardini 2026 - Swarm

Today is day 24 of [Mardini 2026][mardini-2026]. The prompt for today is "Swarm".

Another quick one, since I'm not super excited by this prompt. This was going to be a
stream of fireflies over the ocean, but I couldn't get it to frame them how I wanted,
so eventually it turned into an abstract light show with a ridiculously high
aperture/low f-stop and a custom bokeh.

New stuff:

- Ocean tools, incl.  Houdini Ocean Procedural LOP, and Houdini Preview Procedurals
  LOP.
- Lens shader with strong custom bokeh.

Stars/space HDRI is from https://www.spacespheremaps.com/hdr-spheremaps/.
