### Mardini 2026 - Food

Today is day 21 of [Mardini 2026][mardini-2026]. The prompt for today is "Food".

I had to do something simple because the prompt changed on me, so that means a
stillframe. However, this morning I went to see Project Hail Mary, which meant I'd
be away from my computer for a while so I could start a simulation without actually
wasting my practice time.

So... I set up a dead simple MPM sim for draping the frosting on the bundt cake
after spending most of my time budget this morning just modeling the cake. It's a
pretty simple revolve, but I did some fancy math (mostly trig) to create the cake
grooves/ridges.
