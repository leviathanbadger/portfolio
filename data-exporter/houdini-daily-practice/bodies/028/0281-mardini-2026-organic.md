### Mardini 2026 - Organic

Today is day 18 of [Mardini 2026][mardini-2026]. The prompt for today is "Organic".

The prompt is 'organic'. And it's a pipe organ. Get it?

Seriously though, I just really didn't want do actually model a creature right now.
There's quite a bit of missing geometry in this chapel, but I'm still happy with
what I got done. It did take over four hours, which isn't an insignificant
investment on a weekday...

New stuff:

- Custom planes fed into UV Unwrap SOP.
- Knife SOP. To cut an edge loop on the pipes just below the mouths so that I can
  scale the bottoms without it affecting the radius of the top of the pipes.
- More practice with the Point Instancer LOP for the lighting, although it's a
  placeholder for now.

That's honestly it for new things. Mostly I spent my time using modeling tools
I'm already familiar with here. I do intend to come back to this practice, since
I'm having a lot of fun with it, and I left quite a lot still to do. Including,
but not limited to:

- Sheet music stand and sheet music on back of 'piano'.
- Wood facades in front of pipe bases, so they're not just sitting on the ground.
- Wall decorations, including stained glass windows for lighting. Improved wall
  material(s).
- Arched ceiling/roof.
- Pews.
- Lowered floor for audience, stairs to stage area, half-height wall and podium.
- Change floor from wood to carpet.
- Create grunge/wear on piano keys/sides so it doesn't look as pristine.
- Extrude pipes so that the mouth opening doesn't look impossibly thin.
- Extend room size, add doors at back (or sides). In any case, add some kind of
  geometry detail on back wall so that reflections don't look so flat.
