### Mardini 2026 - Stylized

Today is day 20 of [Mardini 2026][mardini-2026]. The prompt for today is "Stylized".

Here's another quick & dirty practice. This one at least has more effort put into it
than the ['fonts' practice](https://brandonslade.me/houdini-practice/278) from day 15,
but it's mostly to get it out of the way because I'm getting pretty burned out and I'm
out of time to do something more complex anyway. I skipped two days of practice, which
completely burns up my lead again.

New stuff:

- Planar Inflate SOP. Used once before, more than a year ago.
- Inflate SOP - to separate grass from the soccer ball without a full-blown simulation.
  This time used with 'pinned' points so that the base of the grass doesn't move.
  Ultimately the result is barely visible in the output image, and then only if you
  look carefully; but that's fine.
- Quantize COP - used in slap comp to create striations in color by value.
