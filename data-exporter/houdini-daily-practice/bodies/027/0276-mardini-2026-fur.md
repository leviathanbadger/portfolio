### Mardini 2026 - Fur

Today is day 13 of [Mardini 2026][mardini-2026]. The prompt for today is "Fur".

I created most of the scene relatively quickly -- all but the carpet -- but spent
over two hours on attempting to light it the way I want it lit. The problem is that
the Karma Physical Sky *really* wants a soft golden light when the sun is this low;
but I'm not rendering a sunset, I just want a nice looking sun on a normal looking
sky, seen through some windows in a cool-lit house. (Cool in terms of color
temperature.) So I got burned out before I even started trying to create the carpet,
at which point I skipped a day of Houdini practice. So now I'm on the line and can't
afford to let a day slip or I'll 'fail' the challenge. (At least, the practice every
day part of it.)

I was going to try to do something for pi day, but no matter how I thought of it I
couldn't conceive of a practice project that combined 'pi' or 'pie' with 'fur'. Oh,
I had some ideas, but they were all terrible and I didn't do them. You're welcome.

New stuff:

- Portal lights. I've tried this before, but I think I fundamentally misunderstood
  some things about it. The technique is more limited than I thought it would be;
  but after much weeping and gnashing of teeth, I did manage to use this to create
  a room that's lit in a way that's not strictly physically accurate to help drive
  the look I want.
- Hair Generate SOP.
- Karma Procedural: Fur. Well, I tried at any rate. I couldn't get it to actually
  generate the carpet based on the floor 'skin' and generated guide curves, so I
  want back to using the Hair Generate SOP to generate the complete set of carpet
  curves and rendered it directly instead of procedurally. Still, I'm going to give
  myself this one because of the effort, failed as it was.
- Custom (very much not physically real) sun glow. Using COPs/slap comp.
