### Mardini 2026 - Clothing

Today is day 14 of [Mardini 2026][mardini-2026]. The prompt for today is "Clothing".

I was just going to make a hat, then run a Vellum simulation to wrinkle it a bit and
deform it using a flat brim animated into the correct shape. However, the sim, as well
as some complexities getting the stitch pattern into COPs for texture work, caused the
practice to take... about four hours. I really thought this practice would be simple.
It wasn't. So the time I had for rendering was low, and I once again burned myself out
doing it.

New stuff:

- Vellum simulation with 'attach to geometry' constraint.
- Vellum sim with stitch constraint (done once before).
- Rasterize Curve COP, esp. with curves that cross UV seams.

HDRI is from GreyscaleGorilla.
